Our Fire Detection Systems

At Shree Ganesh Enterprises, we offer a comprehensive range of fire detection systems tailored to meet diverse safety requirements. Explore our advanced solutions designed to protect lives and property effectively.

Addressable Detection System

An Addressable Fire Detection System identifies the exact location of a fire event. Each detector or device in the system has an address, allowing the control panel to pinpoint the precise location of a fire. This system is ideal for large buildings and complex structures.

Conventional Detection System

The Conventional Fire Detection System divides the premises into different zones. Each zone has multiple detectors connected to the control panel. When a detector is triggered, the control panel indicates which zone the fire is in, but it does not specify the exact location within the zone.

Wireless Fire Detection System

The Wireless Fire Detection System uses radio signals to communicate between detectors and the control panel. This system is easy to install and ideal for buildings where wiring is difficult, such as historical structures or temporary installations.

Smoke Detection System

Smoke Detection Systems are designed to detect smoke particles in the air, which can be an early sign of a fire. These systems are typically installed in areas where fires are likely to produce smoke before flames, such as in living spaces or office environments.

Heat Detection System

Heat Detection Systems activate when they sense a rise in temperature above a set threshold. These systems are useful in environments where smoke detectors might cause false alarms, such as kitchens or garages.
