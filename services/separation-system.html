Separation System

Our separation systems are designed to protect valuable assets and ensure the safety of personnel by providing advanced fire suppression solutions. These systems are ideal for environments where water-based suppression might not be suitable, offering a variety of alternatives that ensure effective fire control without causing water damage.

Our Separation Systems Include:

  • FM-200 Waterless Fire Protection: The FM-200 system is a waterless fire suppression solution that discharges gas to extinguish fires rapidly. It is particularly suitable for protecting sensitive electronics, data centers, and other environments where water damage could be detrimental.
  • CO2 Gas Flooding System: The CO2 Gas Flooding System is a highly effective method for extinguishing fires in enclosed spaces. It works by displacing oxygen, thereby suffocating the fire without leaving any residue, making it ideal for areas with delicate machinery or equipment.
  • Mechanical Foam Flooding System: The Mechanical Foam Flooding System utilizes foam to suppress fires, particularly those involving flammable liquids. The foam creates a barrier that smothers the fire and prevents re-ignition, making it an excellent choice for industrial settings and fuel storage areas.
  • Water Spray System: The Water Spray System is a specialized fire suppression system that uses a high-pressure water spray to control and extinguish fires. It is particularly effective for cooling surfaces and controlling fires in environments where traditional sprinklers might not be sufficient.
