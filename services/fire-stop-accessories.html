Fire Stop Accessories

Our range of fire stop accessories is designed to enhance fire resistance and containment, helping to prevent the spread of fire and providing essential protection in the event of a fire.

Our Fire Stop Accessories Include:

  • Fire Resistance Paints: These specialized paints are designed to resist high temperatures and prevent the spread of flames. They provide an additional layer of protection to structural elements, helping to maintain the integrity of a building during a fire.
  • Fire Resistance Door: Fire-resistant doors are crucial for containing fires and preventing them from spreading to other parts of a building. These doors are made from fire-resistant materials and are essential for ensuring the safety of occupants and property.
  • Fire Resistance Material: We offer a variety of fire-resistant materials that can be used in construction and renovation projects to enhance fire safety. These materials are designed to withstand high temperatures and prevent the spread of fire.
  • Fire Brick: Fire bricks are specially designed to withstand extreme heat and are commonly used in fireplaces, furnaces, and other high-temperature applications. They provide essential fire resistance and are a key component in fire safety construction.
  • Water Curtain/Fire Curtain: Water and fire curtains are innovative fire protection solutions that create a barrier to prevent the spread of flames and smoke. Water curtains use a continuous stream of water, while fire curtains are made of fire-resistant materials, both serving as critical components in fire containment strategies.
