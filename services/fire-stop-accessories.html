Fire Stop Accessories We Provide

Fire Resistance Paints

Fire Resistance Paints are specially formulated coatings that help to protect structures from fire damage. These paints are designed to maintain their protective qualities under high temperatures, providing critical time for occupants to evacuate and for emergency services to respond.

Fire Resistance Doors

Fire Resistance Doors are designed to prevent the spread of fire and smoke between compartments in a building. They are constructed with materials that can withstand high temperatures, offering essential protection and allowing safe evacuation during a fire emergency.

Fire Resistance Materials

Fire Resistance Materials are used in the construction of buildings to enhance their ability to withstand fire. These materials include fire-resistant boards, insulation, and barriers that help contain and slow down the spread of fire, ensuring structural integrity during an emergency.

Fire Bricks

Fire Bricks are specially manufactured bricks that can withstand high temperatures. They are used in the construction of fireboxes, kilns, and other high-temperature applications to provide a durable and heat-resistant surface that prevents heat damage to surrounding structures.

Water Curtain

A Water Curtain is a fire protection system that creates a barrier of water to prevent the spread of fire and smoke. It is often used in large buildings and industrial facilities to protect critical areas and provide a safety zone during a fire emergency.

Fire Curtain

Fire Curtains are deployed to contain smoke and fire within specific areas of a building. Made from fire-resistant materials, they act as barriers that guide the movement of smoke and provide clear escape routes for occupants during a fire.
