Miscellaneous Equipment

In addition to our core fire detection and suppression systems, we offer a range of miscellaneous equipment designed to enhance fire safety and ensure comprehensive protection in various environments.

Our Miscellaneous Equipment Includes:

  • Clean Agent: Clean Agent systems are fire suppression solutions that use environmentally friendly gases to extinguish fires without leaving any residue. These systems are ideal for protecting sensitive equipment, such as electronics and data centers, as they do not cause damage to the assets they protect.
  • Fire Killer: Fire Killer is a versatile fire suppression tool that can be used to quickly and effectively extinguish small to medium-sized fires. It is portable, easy to use, and suitable for various environments, including homes, vehicles, and workplaces.
