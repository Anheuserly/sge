Electrical Systems

Our comprehensive electrical services cover a wide range of critical infrastructure components that ensure reliable and efficient power distribution for your projects.

Our Electrical Services Include:

  • 11 KV Transformer: We provide high-quality 11 KV transformers that are essential for stepping down high voltage electricity to a usable level for industrial and commercial applications. These transformers ensure stable and efficient power supply.
  • HT/LT Panel: Our HT (High Tension) and LT (Low Tension) panels are designed to manage and distribute electrical power efficiently across various segments of your facility. These panels are critical for maintaining a steady power flow and protecting electrical equipment.
  • HT/LT CB (Circuit Breaker): Our circuit breakers are crucial safety devices that automatically cut off the electrical supply in case of overload or fault, protecting your equipment from damage and ensuring safety.
  • HT/LT Bus Coupler Isolator: These devices are used to isolate electrical circuits for maintenance or in case of a fault, providing flexibility and safety in the power distribution system.
  • Distribution Board Panel: We offer robust distribution boards that efficiently divide electrical power into subsidiary circuits while providing a protective fuse or circuit breaker for each circuit in a common enclosure.
  • D G Set (Diesel Generator Set): Our DG sets provide reliable backup power during outages, ensuring that your operations can continue uninterrupted in case of power failure.
  • Internal Electrification: We specialize in the complete internal electrification of buildings, ensuring safe and efficient distribution of power to all internal areas, including lighting, HVAC, and other electrical systems.
  • External Electrification: Our external electrification services cover the installation and maintenance of power distribution systems outside the building, including street lighting, outdoor power outlets, and other external electrical infrastructure.
  • Power Cable Distribution: We handle the installation and management of power cable networks, ensuring efficient power distribution across your facility with minimal loss and maximum safety.
