Electrical Services

At Shree Ganesh Enterprises, we offer comprehensive electrical services to meet all your power distribution and management needs. Our expertise covers a wide range of electrical systems and components, ensuring reliable and efficient power solutions for your projects.

11KV Transformer

We provide high-quality 11KV transformers for efficient power distribution in various industrial and commercial applications. Our transformers are designed for reliability and optimal performance.

HT/LT Panels

Our HT (High Tension) and LT (Low Tension) panels are custom-designed to meet specific power distribution requirements. We ensure safety, efficiency, and compliance with industry standards.

HT/LT CB (Circuit Breakers)

We offer a range of high-quality circuit breakers for both HT and LT applications, providing essential protection for your electrical systems against overloads and short circuits.

HT/LT Bus Coupler Isolator

Our bus coupler isolators are designed for seamless power transfer between different bus sections, ensuring continuous power supply and system flexibility.

Distribution Board Panel

We design and install distribution board panels that efficiently distribute electrical power throughout your facility, ensuring safe and organized power management.

D G Set (Diesel Generator Set)

Our diesel generator sets provide reliable backup power solutions for various applications, ensuring uninterrupted operations during power outages.

Internal Electrification

We offer comprehensive internal electrification services, including wiring, lighting, and power outlet installations for residential, commercial, and industrial spaces.

External Electrification

Our external electrification services cover outdoor lighting, power distribution, and electrical infrastructure for exterior spaces and large-scale projects.

With our extensive experience and skilled team, we ensure that all electrical installations are completed to the highest standards of safety and efficiency. Contact us today to discuss your electrical service needs.
