AMC

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is a service agreement between Shree Ganesh Enterprises and our clients to provide routine maintenance and support for various systems and equipment. Our AMC services are categorized into two types:

Comprehensive AMC: This type of AMC includes comprehensive maintenance and support services ensuring that all systems are functioning optimally. It covers regular inspections, preventive maintenance, and immediate response to issues to ensure minimal downtime and extended equipment life.

Non-Coprehensive AMC: This type of AMC provides basic maintenance services and support, focusing primarily on corrective actions rather than preventive measures. It is suitable for clients who need occasional support and troubleshooting without the extensive coverage provided by a Coprehensive AMC.

Electrical LT/HT

At Shree Ganesh Enterprises, we specialize in the design, installation, and maintenance of HT (High Tension) and LT (Low Tension) panels and cables, which are essential components in any power distribution system. Our expertise ensures that electricity is safely distributed across buildings, industries, and urban infrastructure.

HT Panels are used for transmitting high voltage electricity from the grid to large-scale industrial and commercial facilities. These panels are built to handle voltages ranging from 11kV to 33kV and are critical for ensuring efficient energy distribution with minimal losses. LT Panels, on the other hand, are designed to manage and distribute low voltage electricity (below 1kV) for residential, commercial, and smaller industrial settings.

Our comprehensive solutions also include high-quality HT and LT cables, ensuring durability, safety, and reliable performance in power distribution networks. We follow strict industry standards to guarantee safety, energy efficiency, and optimal functionality, making our panels and cables a preferred choice in various sectors.

From planning to installation, we deliver tailor-made electrical solutions that meet the power demands of any infrastructure while ensuring compliance with safety standards.
