Separation Systems

Our separation systems are designed to enhance fire containment within buildings. We install fire-resistant barriers, including walls, doors, and partitions, to prevent the spread of fire and smoke. These systems are crucial for maintaining safety and protecting different areas of a building from fire damage.

We provide tailored solutions to meet specific fire safety codes and regulations. Our team also offers fire stopping solutions for penetrations through barriers, ensuring that gaps around pipes and cables are securely sealed. With our comprehensive approach, you can be confident that your separation systems are effective and reliable.

Fire Stop Accessories

Fire stop accessories are vital for maintaining the integrity of fire-rated barriers within a building. We offer a range of products including sealants, fire collars, and intumescent materials designed to seal gaps around pipes, cables, and other penetrations. These accessories prevent the passage of fire and smoke, helping to compartmentalize and contain fires effectively.

Our team ensures that all fire stop accessories meet stringent safety standards and provide reliable protection. We also provide installation and maintenance services to ensure that all fire stop solutions are correctly applied and functioning as intended. Our focus is on delivering products that enhance safety and contribute to effective fire management.
