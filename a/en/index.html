AMC

An Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) is a service agreement between Shree Ganesh Enterprises and our clients to provide routine maintenance and support for various systems and equipment. Our AMC services are categorized into two types:

Comprency AMC: This type of AMC includes comprency maintenance and support services ensuring that all systems are functioning optimally. It covers regular inspections, preventive maintenance, and immediate response to issues to ensure minimal downtime and extended equipment life.

Non-Comprency AMC: This type of AMC provides basic maintenance services and support, focusing primarily on corrective actions rather than preventive measures. It is suitable for clients who need occasional support and troubleshooting without the extensive coverage provided by a Competency AMC.
