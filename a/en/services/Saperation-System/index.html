Separation Systems

FM-200 Waterless Fire Protection

FM-200 Waterless Fire Protection: Our advanced clean agent fire suppression system uses FM-200 gas to quickly extinguish fires without leaving residues. It is ideal for sensitive environments such as data centers where traditional methods might cause damage.

CO2 Gas Flooding System

CO2 Gas Flooding System: This system utilizes carbon dioxide to suppress fires by displacing oxygen, making it effective in areas where water or other suppression agents cannot be used. Perfect for protecting valuable equipment and machinery.

Mechanical Foam Flooding System

Mechanical Foam Flooding System: Produces a foam blanket that cools and suffocates fires, offering superior protection for flammable liquid storage and industrial applications. Ideal for environments where liquids are stored or processed.

Water Spray System

Water Spray System: This system delivers high-pressure water sprays to protect against fires, particularly in high-risk areas. It provides effective cooling and suppression in both indoor and outdoor environments.
